Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a simple act of kindness that has profound impact. Here's why you should donate.

❤️

Save Lives

One donation can save up to three lives including red blood cells, plasma, and platelets.

Health Benefits

Regular blood donation helps reduce risk of heart disease and maintain healthy iron levels.

Quick & Easy

The entire process takes less than an hour and can be done every 3 months.
